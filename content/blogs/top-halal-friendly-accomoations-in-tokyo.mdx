# Top Halal Friendly Accommodations in Tokyo

Can you think of a city with jungles and ancient temples that is also the epitome of modern technology, with mesmerizing skyscrapers and mouth-watering street food? Yes, you guessed it right: I am talking about **Tokyo**.

As a Muslim traveler, you must be concerned about finding Halal-friendly accommodations in the city. The interesting news is that there are a couple of Muslim-friendly accommodations in the town where you can avail yourself of prayer facilities and find Halal food nearby.

There are so many reasons to visit Tokyo, but indulging in the scrumptious Japanese cuisine and enjoying the hustle and bustle of the busy city have to be the top reasons. Let’s not wait further and find out the **top 5 Halal-Friendly accommodations in Tokyo**:

---

## 1. Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo

![Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo](/images/blog/39895262_600x600.webp)

If you want to treat yourself to a luxurious stay with breathtaking views and exceptional customer service, then **Hotel Chinzanso** is the perfect place to stay. The significant feature of this hotel is its exclusive Muslim-friendly services.

This beautiful hotel is located on a hill, and the appealing garden surrounding it is what makes it unique. If you are staying at Hotel Chinzanso, you can avail of prayer mats and a Qibla compass. The spacious rooms of the hotel are equipped with Muslim-friendly cosmetics. You can have the opportunity to try a Muslim-friendly skincare series at the hotel named **VKSARA**, specially designed for Muslim guests.

Apart from this, the hotel’s menu has Halal dishes; you can try Japanese cuisine that is made of Halal ingredients.

**Address:** 2-chōme-10-8 Sekiguchi, Bunkyo City, Tokyo 112-8680, Japan  
**Contact:** +81 3 3943 1111  
**Book Here:** [Link](https://agoda.tp.st/uIOmZkKB)

**Nearby Halal Food:**  
**RESTOURANT** (7-minute ride)  
Halal dishes include kebab sandwiches, kebab don, and kebab wrap.  
**Address:** Japan, 〒 169-0075 Tokyo, Shinjuku City, Takadanobaba, 1 Chome-6-15  
**Opening Hours:** 6 am to 3 am

**Nearby Mosque:**  
**Otsuka Masjid & Japan Islamic Trust** (33-minute walk)  
**Address:** Japan, 〒 170-0005 Tokyo, Toshima City, Minamiotsuka, 3 Chome-42-7  
**Opening Hours:** 4 am to 10 pm
