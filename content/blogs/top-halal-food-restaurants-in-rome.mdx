# Top Halal Food Restaurants in Rome

Rome, the **“Eternal City,”** is a historic place with mesmerizing destinations that leave you in a timeless embrace. As the capital of Italy, it boasts an unparalleled cultural heritage, with landmarks such as the **Colosseum**, **Vatican City**, and the **Pantheon** drawing millions of visitors each year.

After exploring these incredible sites, you’ll want a meal that satisfies your taste buds—and for Muslim travelers, that means **halal food**. Luckily, Rome has an ever-growing number of halal restaurants, offering everything from Middle Eastern shawarma to spicy Indian curries and crispy fried chicken.

Below is a list of the **top halal food restaurants in Rome** to satisfy your cravings.

---

## 1. Shawarma Station

![Shawarma Station](/images/blog/pexels-rdne-5779370-2_480x480.webp)

If you’re in the mood for something beyond Italian, **Shawarma Station** is one of the most popular halal spots in Rome. From falafel and baklava to aromatic biryani and juicy kebabs, they serve up flavorful Middle Eastern dishes at reasonable prices with generous portions. The staff is friendly, and the place is great for dining in, takeout, or delivery.

- **Address:** Via Merulana, 271, 00185 Roma RM, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 06488216
- **Opening Hours:** 10:30 AM - 11:30 PM
- **Cuisine:** Middle Eastern, Lebanese, Mediterranean

---

## 2. Peri Peri Grill

![Peri Peri Grill](/images/blog/pexels-tim-douglas-6210876-2_480x480.webp)

**Peri Peri Grill** is the place to be for a quick, satisfying halal meal. Known for its grilled chicken, Indian curries, pizza, and spaghetti, it’s a solid choice whether you’re starting your day or winding down from sightseeing. The ambiance is welcoming and laid-back.

- **Address:** Via Marghera, 11/B, 00185 Roma RM, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 331 819 2206
- **Opening Hours:** 6:30 AM - 12:30 PM
- **Cuisine:** Italian, Indian, European, British

---

## 3. Ali Baba

![Ali Baba](/images/blog/mix-grill-7414547_1280_480x480.webp)

A well-known Syrian halal restaurant in Rome, **Ali Baba** delivers authentic Middle Eastern cuisine with standout dishes like lamb kebabs, hummus, and falafel. It’s also family-friendly with outdoor seating and vegetarian options. Don’t miss their baklava!

- **Address:** Via dell’Arco di Travertino, 27, 00178 Roma RM, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 331 819 2206
- **Opening Hours:** 10:00 AM - 5:00 AM
- **Cuisine:** Middle Eastern, Turkish, Barbecue

---

## 4. HFC Foods (Halal Fried Chicken)

![HFC Foods](/images/blog/pexels-enginakyurt-2725744-2_480x480.webp)

Craving halal fast food? **HFC Foods** has you covered with burgers, wraps, fried chicken, pasta, and pizza—all halal-certified. It’s great for a quick bite and conveniently located near the Vatican.

- **Address:** Viale Giulio Cesare, 191, 00192 Roma RM, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 06 94326561
- **Opening Hours:** 10:30 AM - 10:30 PM
- **Cuisine:** Asian, Bangladeshi

---

## 5. Ciro Kebab

![Ciro Kebab](/images/blog/indian-5093242_1280-2_480x480.webp)

Located in Cento Celle, **Ciro Kebab** is beloved for its flavorful Turkish and Middle Eastern dishes. Whether you’re ordering lamb shawarma, mixed grills, or falafel wraps, this spot is praised for generous portions and friendly service. Try their espresso too!

- **Address:** Via dei Castani, 251/F, 00172 Roma RM, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 06 24404140
- **Opening Hours:** 11:00 AM - 2:00 AM
- **Cuisine:** Middle Eastern, Turkish

---

## 6. Istanbul Pizza-Kebab

![Istanbul Pizza-Kebab](/images/blog/pizza-5179939_1280_480x480.webp)

**Istanbul Pizza Kebab** blends Turkish, Middle Eastern, and Italian flavors. Choose from pizzas, shawarma, kebabs, and meze dishes like hummus and baba ghanoush. It's perfect for a relaxed meal with a cozy atmosphere reflecting both Turkish and Italian influences.

- **Address:** Viale Giulio Cesare, 70, 00192 Roma RM, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 06 24404140
- **Opening Hours:** 10:00 AM - 1:00 AM
- **Cuisine:** Turkish, Middle Eastern, Pizza

---

Whether you’re a local or a visitor, these halal food spots in Rome offer a delicious and diverse range of options for every palate. From grab-and-go shawarma to rich grilled meats and fast food, you're sure to find a halal meal that hits the spot.
