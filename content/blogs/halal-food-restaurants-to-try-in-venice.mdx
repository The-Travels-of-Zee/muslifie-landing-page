# Top 6 Halal Restaurants in Venice

Venice, the famous city in **Northern Italy**, needs no introduction. With over 13 million visitors annually, this charming city is known for its **spectacular architecture**, **mild climate**, iconic **water canals**, and romantic vibes. What truly defines the Venice experience is its **boat rides through 118 islands**—a must for every traveler.

For **Muslim travelers**, Venice offers a growing number of **halal food options** and even **prayer facilities**. While exploring, always ask restaurant staff about their halal status, as some eateries may serve alcohol or only offer a partially halal menu. Some restaurants are officially certified halal, while others are run by trustworthy Muslim owners.

Thanks to a strong presence of immigrants from **Afghanistan, Syria, Egypt, Lebanon, Jordan, and Bangladesh**, Venice has plenty of Middle Eastern, South Asian, and Indian halal eateries.

Here are the **top 6 halal restaurants in Venice** to check out on your next trip:

---

## 1. Brothers Fast Food & Namaste Indian Restaurant

![Brothers Fast Food](/images/blog/ai-generated-8799843_1280_480x480.webp)

If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, **Brothers Fast Food & Namaste** is a must-visit. They serve a wide variety of halal and vegetarian options with stunning canal-side views. It’s perfect for a full-course Indian meal.

- **Address:** Viale Stazione 6/a, 30171, Mestre, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 041 822 1446
- **Opening Hours:** 10:00 AM - 1:45 AM
- **Cuisine:** Halal Indian, Vegan options

---

## 2. Fairouz

![Fairouz](/images/blog/salad-1672505_1280_480x480.webp)

Looking for a Middle Eastern night out? **Fairouz** is a Lebanese restaurant with an ambient setting, known for its Mediterranean and BBQ dishes. Everything on the menu is halal and sure to impress.

- **Address:** Via Antonio Olivi 53, 30171, Mestre, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 328 299 9303
- **Opening Hours:** 6:00 PM - 12:00 AM
- **Cuisine:** Halal Lebanese, Mediterranean, BBQ

---

## 3. SKK Restaurant

![SKK Restaurant](/images/blog/pizza-386717_1280_480x480.webp)

Popular among budget travelers, **SKK** serves fast food like burgers, sandwiches, pizza, and hot dogs. They also cater to vegetarians with falafel options. Prices start around €8—ideal for casual eats.

- **Address:** Campo Manin, 4253, 30100 Venezia VE, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 041 522 6879
- **Opening Hours:** 9:00 AM - 3:00 AM
- **Cuisine:** Fast Food, Mediterranean

---

## 4. Orient Experience

![Orient Experience](/images/blog/biryani-7009110_1280_480x480.webp)

Located near Santa Margherita Square and the Grand Canal, **Orient Experience** offers a culinary tour of Middle Eastern and South Asian food. Try their Palestinian chicken, Syrian malouf, Afghani dishes, or Bangladeshi veggie curry.

- **Address:** Rio Tera Farsetti Cannaregio 1847/B, 30121 Venice, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 041 822 4337
- **Opening Hours:** 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM
- **Cuisine:** Middle Eastern, Pakistani, Afghan
- **Website:** [orientexperience.it](https://orientexperience.it/)

---

## 5. Ital India

![Ital India](/images/blog/paneer-7043099_1280_480x480.webp)

**Ital India** is often ranked the **#1 halal spot in Venice**, serving authentic North and South Indian cuisine. Run by an Afghan owner and a Punjabi chef, the menu includes Chicken Vindaloo, Lamb Masala, Prawns Karahi, and vegetarian specialties like Paneer Palak.

- **Address:** Fondamenta de le Capuzine, 3102, 30121 Venezia VE, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 041 524 3817
- **Opening Hours:** 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM
- **Cuisine:** Indian, Pakistani, Afghan
- **Facebook:** [Ital India Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/Ital.IndiaRistorante/photos/?paipv=0)

---

## 6. Gibran

![Gibran](/images/blog/meat-1155132_1280_480x480.webp)

**Gibran** is a stylish Lebanese restaurant that caters to both halal and vegetarian/vegan diets. Their offerings include falafel, hummus, moutabal, labneh, and more. Don’t skip their famous Arabic desserts like baklava and cakes.

- **Address:** Calle del Cafetier, 6645, 30122 Venezia VE, Italy
- **Phone:** +39 375 599 7676
- **Opening Hours:** 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- **Cuisine:** Lebanese, Mediterranean, Middle Eastern
- **Instagram:** [Gibran Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/explore/locations/2280305435580326/ristorante-libanese-gibran/)

---

**Conclusion**  
Venice might be known for its gondolas and romance, but its **halal food scene** is a pleasant surprise for Muslim travelers. Whether you crave spicy Indian curry, grilled Lebanese meats, or vegetarian South Asian dishes, there’s something for every palate.
